Hello! I’m Ellie

Since you’ve made it to this part of my website, you probably want to know more about me. I’m not really sure how to talk about myself so I’ll just start with what I’m most proud of; that is my art! Yes! I’m an artist, and all the cool abstract pictures in this website are my own works. I’ve honed my skills and my love for creative expression for well over 10 years.

Being an artist has taught me many things that I continually apply to my daily life and my success!Creativity taught me how to be a problem solver, it also taught me to enjoy the process. Planning big projects taught me how to manage my time, how to keep going even when I don’t see progress right away. It taught me how to be better organized for the future projects. Selling my art taught me how to network! Making connections may be the best thing you can ever do for yourself.

Now I’m midway through coding school and I’m enjoying every bit of it. I’m excited to jump into the world of coding and apply the skills I’ve been taught. I’m also eager to learn new skills along the way. I know I’m just fine working alone, but I hope I can find a team (or many) because I love to help people, and working with a team is very rewarding.
